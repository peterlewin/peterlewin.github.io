What Are Merchant Fees?

Merchant fees are transaction charges imposed when using an eCommerce platform or Point of Sale (PoS) system. These fees, typically paid by business owners, accumulate per sale and can significantly impact profit margins over time.

At eZ-Commerce Solutions, we focus on empowering businesses by increasing functionality while reducing costs. That’s why we offer 0% merchant fees for your preferred payment methods, helping you keep more of what you earn.

However, payment providers like PayPal and banks apply merchant fees regardless of which platform you use. These charges are separate from your eCommerce service and vary depending on the payment gateway. To learn more about PayPal and debit/credit card fees, visit their Merchant Fees page.

By choosing eZ-Commerce Solutions, you can enjoy a seamless and cost-effective online sales experience without additional platform fees.
